Pharma Supply Chain System

Description: The Pharma Supply Chain System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline inventory management, shipment tracking, and order processing for the pharmaceutical industry. It offers real-time monitoring and ensures transparency between users, suppliers, and administrators. By integrating secure authentication and compliance features, the system helps safeguard medicine integrity and combat counterfeiting risks. The platform enhances operational efficiency and accountability across the entire supply chain.
